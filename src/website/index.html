Save Our Future!!




Welcome to the hompe page of 'Save Our Future'.

This is an informative website focused on educating primary school children between the ages of 7 and 13. It gives a basic overview of Climate Change, the causes and the consequences.

How to navigate the website:

1. On the top left of each page is a Home button, which takes you back to this page.

2. On this page that button is a Settings button, this takes you to a settings page where you can change the background colour of this website.

3. To navigate to the educational pages click on its corresponding image below.

4. On the four educational pages you can navigate, using the buttons at the top of the screen, to the previous or next page.

5. Finally, don't forget to take 'The QUIZ'. The link to it is at the bottom of this page or you can click here. The questions are based upon information found on the educational pages.

Things to Know:

1. Each educational page has key words, highlighted in this colour!

2. There are interactive graphs on each educational page which you can click on to see the data in different ways.

3. At the bottom of each eduducational page there is a frightening fact. These facts are the questions on the Quiz at the end! Other than that Enjoy!!
